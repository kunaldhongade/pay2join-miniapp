## üîë Key Takeaways for Your Feedback

### What Makes Good Feedback:

1. **Be Specific**
   - ‚ùå "Documentation is unclear"
   - ‚úÖ "The gas estimation page jumps into TVM details without explaining why gas estimation matters"

2. **Provide Context**
   - Include what you were trying to do
   - Show where you got stuck
   - Explain the impact

3. **Suggest Solutions**
   - Don't just identify problems
   - Propose specific improvements
   - Explain the impact

4. **Include Evidence**
   - Screenshots of issues
   - Links to documentation
   - Code examples

5. **Compare with Others**
   - How does TON compare to Ethereum/Solana?
   - What can TON learn from others?
   - What does TON do better?

## üìù Feedback Structure Checklist

When writing your feedback, ensure you include:

- [ ] **Task description** - What you were trying to do
- [ ] **Persona** - Your persona (Novice/Nomad/TON Pro)
- [ ] **Documentation used** - Links with ratings
- [ ] **What worked well** - Positive feedback
- [ ] **Issues found** - Specific problems with details
- [ ] **Screenshots** - Visual evidence
- [ ] **Evaluation scores** - UX, Clarity, Factual Accuracy (1-5)
- [ ] **Comparison** - With other ecosystems
- [ ] **Bugs found** - With steps to reproduce
- [ ] **Suggestions** - Specific, actionable improvements
- [ ] **Code examples** - If applicable
- [ ] **Priority levels** - High/Medium/Low for each suggestion
